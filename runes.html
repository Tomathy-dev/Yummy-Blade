Runes

Runes give champions bonus stats. A single runes offers little to no effect, but it's the culmination of many runes that have a significant impact. That is why, for each rune page you can pick 30 runes. Runes can be purchased in the store for IP, and placed in both Rune Pages every person starts with, in the summoner profile.During champ select you can pick the rune page that most benefits you and through the use of RP can buy more rune pages to your liking.


Let's explore the different categories of runes and what can they offer:

Marks (Reds)- provide the following: attack damage, attack speed, lethality or magic penetration.

Glyphs (Blues)- provide the following: ability power, mana, cooldown eduction or magic resist.

Seals (Yellows)- provide the following: armor, health. health regeneration or mana regeneration.

Quintessences (Quints)- Extra-powerfull runes that provide stats from all categories, along with unique stats like: life-steal or movement speed.


Until level 20, you'll only be able to purchase tier 1 or tier 2 runes, which have reduced effectiveness, but only cost 1 IP each. Once you get to level 20, tier 3 runes are unlocked, which have maximum effectiveness but can cost up to thousands of IP.
